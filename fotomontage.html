Realistic Solar Farm Visualization Software Procedure

During the summer of 2022, I worked as an intern at Solkompaniet, a solar company based in Stockholm, Sweden. While I was there, a large part of my work was to create an in-house procedure to make photorealistic visualizations of how their solar farms would look after installation. My procedure helped Solkompaniet save significantly on the consuting that they used to do for these images.

Project Details

  • Start date: May 2022
  • Date of Completion: July 2022
  • Purpose: Internship
  • Software used:
