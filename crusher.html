Double Toggle Rock Crusher

My rock crusher crushing a granite pebble

Concept & Specs

Using an eccentric cam controlling a 4 bar mechanism (see below), a heavy flywheel, and a small gear ratio, a simple motor is able to provide enough force between two steel plates to crush rocks. This was created throughout the fall of my junior year, with my Machine Elements & Manufacturing Processes (MENG 325) class. I built this alongside a template, but manufactured all of the parts myself (except the bearings).

See progress pics below!
