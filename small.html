Small Projects

While working at the Eli Whitney Museum and Workshop, I spent time designing and developing new projects to be built by kids in our summer camps.

3D Zoetropes

One program I helped to develop explored early forms of animation, inspired by some of Eadweard Muybidge's work 150 years ago. Using simple pulleys and an optical coupler, we created a simple zoetrope, and explored some of the ways that 3D objects could be simply animated. See some examples below. December, 2018

Spinning Tops

Another progrom explored various forms of the simple spinning top. By taking influence from different cultures, children in this program were able to see how kids around the world played with their own twist on the top. April, 2021
