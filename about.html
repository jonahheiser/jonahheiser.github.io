About Me

I grew up in Hamden, CT and attended Hamden High School. I gained my interest in Engineering and Design while working as an apprentice at the Eli Whitney Museum and Workshop, starting at age 13.

Now, I am a junior in Yale College, and am pursuring my interest in sustainable design. I have worked in the green energy field during the past two summers.

For many years of my life, I have been interested in rock climbing. At Yale, I am heavily involved in the Outdoor Climbing Team, and am now the president. See some of my favorite pictures below!
