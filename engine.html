Sterling Engine

This project was for my Thermodynamics class in the spring of 2023. The assignment was to create a working sterling engine from "scraps." I was one of only two students to succeed.

How it works

A sterling engine reapeats four actions to a gas inside a chamber (air in my case), to create conitnuous motion. These four actions are heating, expansion, cooling, and compression. My engine works with a heat source at the bottom of the can, and cold source at the top. A balloon on the top controls the expansion and compression, and a steel wool piston inside the can moves the air between the top (cold) and bottom (hot).
